We Are Tailgater's Flame

In a cozy backyard, friends and family gathered around a smoky grill, savoring delicious food while cheering for their favorite teams. This was the birthplace of Tailgater's Flame, founded by Liam, Skylar, Kenzie, a dynamic trio with a shared passion for food and sports.

Today, Tailgater's Flame is more than a restaurant; it's a community. It's where friends become family, strangers become friends, and every game day feels like a victory. The story of Tailgater's Flame reminds us that a simple idea, fueled by passion and shared with love, can transform into something truly exceptional.
